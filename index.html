Review of my favourite movies!

Wish Dragon
Excellent movie, I really enjoyed it.

When I first heard of this movie, I've always wanted to see this one, since it's from Sony Pictures Animation, the studio who also brought us "The Mitchells vs. The Machines" this year (which I actually really enjoyed a lot) and I'm looking forward to this one. The bad thing though... it's on Netflix, and I don't have Netflix anymore, but eh, whatever. This 21st century Chinese version of "Aladdin" takes us to modern-day Shanghai where Din, a hard-working college student, meets a wish dragon who grants him three wishes in order to find his childhood friend Lina, who's living a life of luxury (sad backstory that both her and Din never met each other again since childhood). I LOVE the film's animation, it's so vibrant and colorful, also the characters' very expressive movements are very fluid like Sony's other works of art. The characters are great (my favorite being Din, and I also like the wish dragon, he's hilarious), the humor's really funny, the chemistry between Din & Long is entertaining, and Din & Lina's is quite sweet to watch (at first I thought they were going to fall in love but then I realized they just want to be friends). I also like how the film deals with socioeconomic status (like whether you're rich or poor) and well, wishes. This is a great example of how to do animated films today the right way, without any of Hollywood's propaganda. And I'm really glad Sony granted me this wish of a movie, and knocked it out of the park again with this one.
